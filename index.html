What is Triathlon

Introduction

triathlon is a multisport endurance race consisting of swimming, cycling, and running segments, typically completed in that order.

History of Triathlons

Triathlons originated in the 1970s gaining momentum with the inaugural Mission Bay Triathlon in 1974. Since then, the sport has evolved, with iconic events like the Ironman World Championship in Hawaii and the Olympic triathlon

Types of Triathlons

There are different types of triathlon such as sprint, Olympic, half-Ironman, and Ironman distances. Each has different distance for each segments

Why Triathlon

Engage in triathlons push your limits physically and mentally, experiencing the thrill of conquering three disciplines in one race Self-discovery, resilience, and camaraderie, as you join a community of individuals committed to pursuing their personal best.
